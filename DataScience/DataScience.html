About The Workshop

Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT (BHU) is going to organise one-day workshop on “A Foundation program on Data Science”. It is designed to provide participants with the concepts and experience necessary to begin with solving data exploration, data preparation, analysis, predictive modelling & deployment problems using Data Science software Statistica. The course will focus on providing hands-on experience with the different ways in which each of these data driven problems can be solved using Data science. For each topic, step-by-step examples will be presented. Research scholars and postgraduate students of the Institute can enroll for the workshop free-of-cost on first-come-first-serve basis.
